INTERSTED IN ESPORTS?

Are you a Scramento State University student? Have you ever thought of taking your gaming to the next level? If so, Sacramento State University has recently added an Esports Club to compete with other Universities in Electronic Sports. The teams consist of Overwatch, League of Legends, Rocket Leage, Halo Infinite, Super Smash Bros. Ultimage and Valorant. If you are interested in joining a team, click on the "join our team" button on the top of the page.
